Upshift Youth Empowerment Initiative (USYEI) is a community-based organisation dedicated to the holistic development of underprivileged and marginalised children, youth, and young women in the rural communities of Uganda. Our Cradle-to-Career approach of comprehensive services supports young people at every stage of their growth.

Through coordinated strategic programs in dance & motivation, creative arts therapy, and food & nutrition, we strive to mitigate the devastating effects of poverty, pave the way for sustainable lifelong success, and empower Uganda's youth to become agents of positive change in their communities.

USYEI takes a comprehensive, community-driven approach to transforming the lives of children and young people. Our approach ensures sustained support at every stage of life through our key pillars.

Learn More About Us

Our Cradle-to-Career (C2C) Approach

USYEI takes a comprehensive, community-driven approach to transforming the lives of children, youth, and young women. Our Cradle-to-Career approach ensures sustained support at every stage of life through four key pillars: Dance & Motivation, Creative Art Therapy, Food & Nutrition, and Community Empowerment.

We invest in the whole individual, tailoring programs to address the unique needs of the communities we serve — from early childhood development to career readiness and economic independence. By integrating health, education, skills development, and leadership opportunities, we create a pathway for lifelong success, breaking cycles of poverty and inequality.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To bring real and lasting change to children, youth and women living in poverty through holistic multidimensional programs in education, economic development, leadership and public health.

Our Vision

A world without poverty where all children and young women are empowered, have meaningful opportunities to grow, learn & lead so that they may reach their full potential.
