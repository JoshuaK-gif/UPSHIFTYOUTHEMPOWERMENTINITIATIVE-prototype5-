Upshift Youth Empowerment Initiative (USYEI) exists to create more access to quality education and life changing opportunities for young people 10-24 in rural communities in Buikwe District by providing Practical skills, mentorship, entrepreneurship training and SRHR education, so they can become self reliant, confident and able to transform their own future.

Through coordinated strategic programs in education, mentorship, health awareness and practical skilling we strive to mitigate the devastating effects of poverty and empower Uganda's youth to become agents of positive change in their communities.

USYEI takes a comprehensive, community-driven approach to transforming the lives of children and young people. Our approach ensures sustained support at every stage of life through our key pillars.

Learn More About Us

Our Seed to Summit (S2S) Approach

Upshift Youth Empowerment Initiative takes a comprehensive, community-driven approach to transforming the lives of rural children and youth. Our From Seed to Summit Approach provides sustained support at every stage of life through four key pillars: Health, Education, Employment, and Empowerment.

We invest in an individual, tailoring programs to meet the unique needs of the communities we serve—from early childhood development to career readiness and economic independence. By integrating health, education, skills development, and leadership opportunities, we create pathways for lifelong success, breaking cycles of poverty and inequality while empowering youth and communities to thrive.

Our Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To empower youth with skills, mentorship, and real opportunities that make them self-reliant and builders of their communities.

Our Vision

To create a future where young people are equipped, confident, and empowered to overcome challenges, drive change, and build resilient, thriving communities.

Meet Our Team
