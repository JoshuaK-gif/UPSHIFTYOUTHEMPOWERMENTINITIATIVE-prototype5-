Program 01

Dance & Motivation Therapy

Children from Buikwe — a place far from the town's exposure — dance with joy, their faces lighting up with happiness, challenging the stereotype that dance is only for those who "fail." Upshift Youth Empowerment Initiative harnesses the transformative power of dance to bring hope and opportunity, while also addressing complex social issues, fostering community engagement, and inspiring action.

"Upshift is my happy place because I dance with the hope of becoming something big in the future."

Program 02

Creative Art Therapy

Children reached in Buikwe District

In Uganda, Creative Arts are often underestimated in schools — with many unaware of their critical role in a child's development. Research shows arts education has a lasting positive impact, enhancing academic achievement, social skills, emotional well-being, and future economic opportunities.

Upshift provides children in Buikwe with access to a safe, supportive space where they can explore painting, drawing, sculpting, and crafting — unlocking their potential and nurturing self-expression.

Program 03

Food & Nutrition

60% of families in Buikwe face food insecurity

In Uganda, malnutrition steals more than health — it robs children of their future and traps families in poverty. Our feeding program provides nourishment, hope, and a chance for children to grow, learn, and thrive.

Every meal is a step toward breaking the cycle of hunger and giving families a brighter tomorrow.
